Introduction:

SialConnect is the platform integrating almost all the empowering industries of the Sialkot, the city of Iqbal. SialConnect website is created by the University students for their final year project but popular due to its uniqueness. It is your gateway to Sialkot's manufacturing excellence. Discover world-class factories and products from Pakistan's industrial hub.

In SialConnect, there is the information of almost all industries of Sialkot including the major industries like surgical, sport, textile industries and many leather industries. This platform also gives contact no, email, website link and physical location of the industries as well. The additional feature include the rating of industries and their products. The user can also check the new arrivals in industries and their production as well in the market after logging in the website.
